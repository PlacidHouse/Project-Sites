Enamel Pins

As the popularity of enamel pins were peaking (#pingame), these were created to balance what was seen as a Simpsonification of the scene. The minimalistic gold on white enamel created a clean contrast to the maximal trends of the moment.


At its best, the enamel pin scene created tiny sculptures worthy of more than denim jackets and bags. These examples weren't meant to go that far, but they were meant to straddle the line between fashion and history. Examples show true to size.
