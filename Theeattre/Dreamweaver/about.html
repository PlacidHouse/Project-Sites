About

THEEATTRE is a digital sketchbook spanning 2013 to 2015. It was inspired by contemporary imagery and pop culture, incorporating ideas, palettes, and symbolism relevant to the current music, art and fashion of the time. The idea was to build a loose portfolio of concepts based on an idealistic interpretation and present them as a visual landscape. Here are a handful of examples from this period.
